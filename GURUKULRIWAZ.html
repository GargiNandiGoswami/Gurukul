THE ANCIENT TEACHING PRACTICES

OVERVIEW

Teaching practices based on gurukul system with the touch of Modern Education practices, which helps in the development of the child's growth and skills develpment.In a contemporary GURUKUL SYSTEM, the teachers play the dual role of parents and mentors. The teachers get a chance to personally connect with students and make them understand the importance of education.They help in the betterment of understanding subjects which enables them to take a genuine interest in the curriculum. The students learn from the guru and help the guru in his everyday life, including carrying out of mundane daily household chores. However, some scholars suggest that the activities are not mundane and very essential part of the education to inculcate self-discipline among students. Typically, a guru does not receive or accept any fees from the shishya studying with him as the relationship between a guru and the shishya is considered very sacred.
